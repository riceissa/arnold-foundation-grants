Arnold Ventures funds various types of projects to understand problems and identify policy solutions. We support 501(c)3 organizations that are focused on Criminal Justice, Education, Health, Public Finance, Research, and Evidence-Based Policy. Below is a listing of all Arnold Ventures C3 grants funded by the Laura and John Arnold Foundation (LJAF) and the Arnolds’ donor-advised fund.
